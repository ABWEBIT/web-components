Portal

A root-level DOM node for modals, overlays, and dialogs.

Info

Place just before the closing </ui-root> tag.

Avoids z-index conflicts, and guarantees that modals, overlays, and dialogs render above all other content.

Placement

<body>
  <ui-root>
    <ui-focus></ui-focus>
    <ui-app></ui-app>
    <ui-portal></ui-portal>
  </ui-root>
</body>
