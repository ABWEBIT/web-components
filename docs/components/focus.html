Focus

A root-level DOM node used to capture focus.

Info

Place right after the opening <ui-root> tag.

Remains invisible and non-interactive to prevent unexpected focus jumps.

Placement


<body>
  <ui-root>
    <ui-focus></ui-focus>
    <ui-app></ui-app>
    <ui-portal></ui-portal>
  </ui-root>
</body>
